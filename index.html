The story

Boys Over Flowers (Hana yori Dango) is a popular Japanese girls' manga (comic) series written and illustrated by Yoko Kamio. The mamga is set in Tokyo, Japan and the fictional elite Eitoku Academy a school for the children of the wealthy. The story is about Tsukushi Makino, a girl who comes from a middle class family, who is enrolled in Eitoku Academy by her mother who wants to compete with the families from her husband's company. While Makino is attending Eitoku she encounters the infamous F4, a group of four handsome young men who are the children of Japan's wealthiest families. The F4 bully anyone who gets in their way.

Boys over Flowers was launched in October 1992 and ran until January 2004. Kamio released an epilogue issue in June 2008. The North American translation was launched in August 2003 and ran until October 2009.
The manga has been adapted into various mediums, some before the manga itself had finished serialization. It was first adapted as an audio drama released on CD from July 1993 to July 1994. This was followed by a live-action feature film in 1995, then an anime television series, produced by Toei Animation, that aired on ABC in 1996.
